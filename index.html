Our Mission

To pursue the higher calling of Jesus Christ by pressing toward the mark (Philippians 3:14); proclaiming the Gospel; cultivating prayer, worship, and discipleship; and serving our neighbors with compassion. We welcome everyone—reflecting the beauty of the Lord’s house, with a heart for our communities.
